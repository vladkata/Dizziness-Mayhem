Dizziness Mayhem is a unique game combining elements of obstacle parkour with simulator style progression. Here's how to begin your adventure:

Obby Biomes

Explore four unique biomes, each with different challenges and obstacles. Each biome has its own unique appearance and weather!

Grass Land

The first biome, perfect for beginners. Learn basic movements and game mechanics while navigating through green meadows and bright forests.

Snow Land

Snowy peaks and slippery ice platforms require precise movement and balance. Watch out for falling snowballs and hidden crevasses!

Desert Land

Hot sands and quick sandstorms increase the challenge. Use sand dunes for acceleration and beware of rotating traps hidden beneath the surface.

Lava Land

The most dangerous biome with bubbling lava and fire eruptions. Only the most experienced players will manage to cross this burning terrain.
